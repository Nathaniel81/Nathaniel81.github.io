StudyMate

StudyMate is a user-friendly study app designed to help students stay organized, motivated, and connected in their learning journey. With StudyMate, you can join a supportive community of learners, set and track learning goals, access a library of educational resources, and collaborate with fellow students. Take your learning experience to the next level with StudyMate.

Key Features

Room Creation

Create and Customize Study Rooms

Create your own study rooms by filling out a simple form. Specify the room name, topics of study, and provide a brief description. This feature allows you to create personalized study environments and connect with like-minded learners.

Room Search

Find the perfect Study Room

With this advanced search functionality, you can easily discover the ideal study room for your needs. Simply enter relevant keywords or filters in the search bar, such as subject, topic, or room name, and browse through a wide range of study rooms that match your criteria. Enhance your learning experience by connecting with peers in dedicated study environments.

Live Chat and Messaging

Connect and Collaborate with Peers

Stay engaged and connected with other learners through this live chat and messaging feature. Once you join a study room, you can instantly communicate with fellow students, exchange ideas, ask questions, and collaborate on projects. Enhance your learning experience by actively participating in discussions, sharing resources, and supporting each other's academic journey.

About Me

StudyMate was inspired by my personal journey as a learner. Throughout my education, I faced challenges in staying organized and motivated. To address this, I created StudyMate as a part of my portfolio project during my time at Holberton School. It aims to provide a user-friendly and supportive platform for students to enhance their learning experience.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School. You can find the source code on GitHub.
